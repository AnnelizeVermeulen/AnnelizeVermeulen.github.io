About me

I am a freelance copywriter with experience in internal communications across a wide range of industries and different media. After starting my writing career in 2000 at Santam, the leading South African short-term insurer, I moved to London in 2003.

During my 11 years living in the UK, I completed London-based roles at the Royal Bank of Scotland (RBS), PayPal, KPMG and Barclays Capital.

Key strengths: Good business acumen; excellent communicator; quick to learn/comprehend; accurate writer and editor; good team player; creative; ability to influence people at various levels. I took a career break after starting a family in 2010 and after returning to Cape Town in 2014, I am ready to continue with my career.
